Description

Salmon is a flavorful, nutrient-rich fish prized for its tender texture and high omega-3 fatty acid content. Whether grilled, baked, or pan-seared, salmon makes for a healthy and elegant main dish.

This recipe keeps things simple with fresh herbs, lemon, and garlic, allowing the natural richness of the salmon to shine. It’s quick to prepare and perfect for both weeknight dinners and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets (about 6 oz each)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon fresh dill (or parsley), chopped
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.
  3. Place salmon fillets on the sheet and drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Rub garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and pepper evenly over the fillets.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until salmon flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Sprinkle with fresh dill or parsley before serving.
