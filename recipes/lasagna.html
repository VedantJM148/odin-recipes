Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Each bite combines savory flavors and tender pasta sheets baked to perfection. This dish is hearty, filling, and perfect for family dinners or gatherings.

Traditionally made with a slow-cooked ragù, lasagna can also be adapted with vegetables, different cheeses, or even made vegetarian. It’s a versatile recipe that rewards patience with incredible flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 2 cups tomato sauce (or canned crushed tomatoes)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic until softened.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if necessary.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20–25 minutes.
  6. In a small bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg and mix well.
  7. Lightly grease a baking dish and spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom.
